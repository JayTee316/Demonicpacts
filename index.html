📈 Skill Coverage
👹 Echo Bosses — All 9 Confirmed
Fight the original boss to receive an Echo Orb. Use it to access the harder demonic variant with unique rewards. Guaranteed unique drop above a kill threshold. Defeating Echo Bosses also unlocks Pact Tree resets.
☠ Demonic Pacts — System Rules
Demonic Pacts are combat masteries — complementary to relics, focused purely on combat. The tree has 130+ nodes, you allocate 40 Pact Points total. The tree can be fully reset 3 times by completing unique Echo Bosses.

Confirmed Pacts

⚔ Melee Pact
Melee attack range doubled while using two-handed weapons.
Melee2H weapons only
🔮 Magic Pact
Heal 1 HP per water rune regenerated when casting combat spells.
MagicWater rune spells
🏹 Ranged Pact
Bows attack 1 tick faster. Does not apply to crossbows.
RangedBows only
